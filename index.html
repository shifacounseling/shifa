About Shifa Counseling

Providing compassionate, evidence-based mental health care

A Safe Space for Healing

At Shifa Counseling, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality mental health care. Our name "Shifa" means healing in Arabic, reflecting our commitment to helping you find peace, understanding, and growth.

At Shifa Counseling we provide Multi Cultural/ Multi Lingual compassionate, personalised mental health care to support your journey towards healing and well-being. Our dedicated team is here to navigate through lifes challenges and foster lasting impact. Our evidence-based approaches are tailored to meet your unique needs and goals.
